Print Project

For the Print project, I decided to create a small lookbook consisting some of my favorite dishes from around the world.

Some of the dishes I chose are Dragon Roll, Tongkotsu Ramen, and Kofta Kebab.

Dragon Roll

I love the flavor and texture of the sushi roll!

Tongkotsu Ramen

This instantly became my favorite the moment I ate the noodles along with the meat and broth! It gets even better when it's spicy!

Kofta Kebab

I've never tried lamb meat in my life, but the juicy flavor and texture of it makes me want to crave more. It's even better with rice.
